What Our Customers Say

Ross Lee

"Indigo has quickly become one of my favorite dining spots in the city. The ambiance is chic and modern, with a lovely mix of cozy and elegant elements. The service was impeccable—our server was attentive without being intrusive. I tried the Pan-Roasted Herb Chicken, and it was cooked to perfection with a delightful blend of herbs. The Chocolate Fondant for dessert was a divine finish to the meal. I highly recommend Indigo for a special night out or a casual dinner with friends. Definitely worth a visit!"

Amelia Watson

"I recently dined at Indigo and was impressed by both the food and the atmosphere. The Seared Tuna Tartare was a standout appetizer—fresh, flavorful, and beautifully presented. For my main course, I had the Crispy Skin Salmon, which was perfectly cooked and paired wonderfully with the quinoa and spinach. The only downside was that the service was a bit slow, but the staff was friendly and accommodating. The Lemon Lavender Cheesecake was a refreshing end to the meal. Overall, Indigo is a great spot with a menu that offers something for everyone."

Ben Roy

"Indigo offers a stylish dining experience with a creative menu. The Truffle Mushroom Arancini were a highlight—crispy on the outside and creamy on the inside. However, the Pan-Roasted Herb Chicken was a bit underwhelming, as it lacked seasoning and was drier than expected. The Berry Mint Cooler was a refreshing non-alcoholic option and the Chocolate Fondant was delicious. The ambiance is great for a night out, but the service could use some improvement as we experienced a few delays. Overall, it’s a decent spot with potential for a fantastic meal."
